Campus Life

school gym

established in 1977

Sport is held in the gym and it is very fun. The gym is also used for any athletics or sport tournaments and can also be used at lunch and snack.



school libary

established in 1977

The library is open to students of all ages to study and read. The opening hours of the library is before school 8 00 - 8 30 and after school it is 3 00 - 4 00 it is also open at lunch from 1 20 - 1 50. The library offers computers to do your work on and has various books it also has a wish list for books that aren't there that you want.



school theatre

established in 1977

The theatre can be used for concerts, performances, band practice and events. It is giant and has pullout theatre seats. on a night where they are performing it looks amazing. as you can see there is also a light system which also looks great
